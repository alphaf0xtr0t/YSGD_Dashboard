A small mountain county, an outsized year

Routt County (Steamboat Springs and the Yampa Valley) shows what's possible when a community rallies around the campaign. With a population of just 25,000 — less than 0.5% of Colorado — Routt's youth sports nonprofits more than tripled their fundraising in 2025, going from $82,000 to nearly $300,000 in two weeks.

Their growth wasn't driven by adding many new organizations (just 9 → 11), but by deeper community engagement per org. It's a model for how rural and resort counties can punch well above their weight on YSGD.
